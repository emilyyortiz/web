True Beauty

True Beauty is a romance Webtoon penned by Yaongyi. The story follows a teen girl who transforms from "ugly" to a "goddess" using makeup, and the two boys she becomes friends with. The first episode in English was published in August 2018, and the webtoon is still ongoing as of February 2021. With a large amount of support from fans, work started on a K-Drama adaptation of the popular webtoon in late 2020. 16 episodes were made, with the first one airing on December 9, 2020.

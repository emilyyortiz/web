True Beauty K-Drama Main Characters

  • Jugyeong Lim
  • Suho Lee
  • Seojun Han

Jugyeong Lim

Jugyeong Lim, played by Moon Ga-young, loves hanging out at her local comic shop to read horror comics, and she listens to alternate rock too. After being bullied at her old school for being ugly, Jugyeong teaches herself makeup from online tutorials and becomes the pretty girl at her new school, Saebom High School. Jugyeong is popular and makes many friends, but she also struggles to keep her real face and hobbies secret.

Suho Lee

Suho Lee (Cha Eun-woo) is very studious and at the top of the class. Suho is rich too, because his dad is a famous actor. It is rumored that he used to be friendly, but after the death of his best friend Seyeon Yoon, Suho has become cold and expressionless. He only talks when necessary and isolates himself, refusing to make friends. However, after meeting Jugyeong, Suho opens up a little bit and shows his softer side. They both like reading horror comics!

Seojun Han

Hwang In-yeop plays the cool and dashing Seojun Han, a former idol trainee. He and Suho used to be best friends too, but they had a falling out after Seyeon passed away. Seojun acts tough in front of others, with his group of friends following him around, but on the inside he is sensitive and caring. Seojun is poor, especially compared to Suho, and he works hard to support his mom and sister. Many girls are attracted to Seojun because of his looks, but the only one he cares for is Jugyeong.
